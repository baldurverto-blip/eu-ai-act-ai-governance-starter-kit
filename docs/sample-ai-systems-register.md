# Sample AI Systems Register

> **Purpose:** Free sample from the EU AI Act AI Governance Starter Kit.
> **Why this sample:** It shows the level of structure used to document ordinary business AI usage without claiming legal or compliance outcomes by itself.
> **Not legal advice.** This is a practical documentation example for small EU teams.

---

## How to use this sample

Use this sample to evaluate whether the toolkit is concrete enough for your team.

Look for:

- clear system ownership
- named business purpose
- visible data categories
- review cadence
- simple, practical control notes

If your team could adapt this register as a real first-pass inventory this week, the full toolkit will likely be a fit.

## Notes on risk labels

- **Ordinary:** Internal drafting, summarization, coding assistance, and research support with limited sensitive data exposure.
- **Elevated:** Customer-facing workflows, people-process content, or outputs that require more deliberate review before use.
- **High:** Use cases involving legal, employment, credit, healthcare, biometric, safety-critical, or similarly sensitive decisions.

High-risk or regulated deployments need specialist review beyond this toolkit.
