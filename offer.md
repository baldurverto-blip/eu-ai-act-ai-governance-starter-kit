## What problem this solves

Many small teams are already using AI, but almost nothing important is written down:

- no shared internal rules
- no named owners
- no clear list of AI tools and use cases
- no lightweight review workflow for customer-facing output
- no incident trail
- no basic vendor diligence record

This pack gives a team a first documentation layer they can adapt now instead of waiting until AI usage is messy, distributed, and difficult to govern.

## What the buyer gets

The purchase includes these editable files in Markdown format:

1. **`AI-usage-policy.md`**
   Internal rules for approved use, prohibited behavior, confidentiality boundaries, review expectations, and escalation triggers.

2. **`ai-systems-register-template.md`**
   A working register for AI tools, owners, business purpose, data categories, review dates, and current controls.

3. **`internal-review-sop.md`**
   A standard operating procedure for human review of AI-assisted output before it reaches customers, candidates, or important decisions.

4. **`incident-log-template.md`**
   A log for hallucinations, misuse, policy breaches, data exposure concerns, root causes, and remediation actions.

5. **`vendor-review-checklist.md`**
   A lightweight diligence template for external AI vendors, model providers, and embedded assistants.

6. **`README-deliverables.md`**
   A walkthrough of how the documents fit together, how to adapt them in the first week, and where the kit stops.
