# AI Governance Starter Kit - Product Rebuild Plan

## Current Problems (Honest Assessment)
1. **DOCX files** - placeholder headlines, no real policy content
2. **XLSX** - fake sample data, no real tracking infrastructure
3. **PPTX** - bullet points without real presentation value
4. **No business value** - not usable for any real company

## What Needs to be Done

### Phase 1: Research & Planning

#### 1.1 Understand EU AI Act Requirements
- Map which requirements apply to small EU businesses
- Identify mandatory vs recommended governance elements
- Understand risk categories and compliance needs

#### 1.2 Define Real Product Scope
- Target audience: Small EU teams using AI (not enterprises)
- Delivery format: Professional Office documents
- Price point: €149

#### 1.3 Research What Good Looks Like
- Find real AI governance policy examples
- Identify industry standards for AI documentation
- Understand what makes documentation useful

### Phase 2: Build Proper Content

#### 2.1 AI Usage Policy Document
Must include:
- Scope and definitions
- Approved AI tool categories (with examples)
- Prohibited uses with rationale
- Employee responsibilities
- Data handling requirements
- Security guidelines
- Compliance requirements (EU AI Act reference)
- Review and update procedures
- Incident response procedures

#### 2.2 AI Systems Register (XLSX)
Must include columns for:
- Tool name and vendor
- Purpose/use case
- Data access level (none, low, medium, high)
- Risk assessment
- Approval status
- Review date
- Compliance status
- Owner/responsible person
- Date added/deactivated
- Notes

#### 2.3 Vendor Assessment Checklist (XLSX)
Must include:
- Security questionnaire
- Data handling policies
- GDPR compliance check
- Incident response capabilities
- Service level agreements
- Cost and licensing

#### 2.4 Incident Response Log (XLSX)
Must include:
- Date of incident
- AI tool involved
- Incident type
- Description
- Severity assessment
- Resolution steps taken
- Prevention measures

#### 2.5 Implementation Guide
- How to use the documents
- Timeline for implementation
- Training requirements
- Review schedule

#### 2.6 Internal SOP
- Review frequency
- Approval workflows
- Escalation procedures

### Phase 3: Quality Checks

- Cross-reference with EU AI Act requirements
- Ensure consistency between documents
- Verify all placeholders are logical
- Add professional formatting

## Research Sources to Use
1. EU AI Act official documentation
2. Industry AI governance best practices
3. ISO standards for AI management
4. GDPR requirements for AI systems
